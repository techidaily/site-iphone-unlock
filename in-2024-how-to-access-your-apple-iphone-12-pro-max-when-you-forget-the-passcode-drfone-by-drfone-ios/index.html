One of the most frequent causes of the Apple ID password prompts is when your Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max has undergone a software update. The update may require you to re-verify your Apple ID credentials to ensure security and privacy.

If the settings are not in sync with your Apple ID credentials or if there are inconsistencies, it can trigger repeated password prompts.

If there was an issue when you purchased or downloaded something, your Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max might prompt you to verify your Apple ID to complete the purchase.

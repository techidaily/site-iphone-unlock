## Part 1. Major Threats To Consider When iPhone Is Stolen

A lost iPhone can become susceptible to a myriad of threats. It could compromise your privacy, security, and personal data. Here are the reasons why learning **how to lock your Apple iPhone 7 when lost** is essential:

1. **Unauthorized Access to Personal Data:** Thieves can exploit an unsecured Apple iPhone 7 to gain unauthorized access to personal information. These include contacts, messages, emails, and photos.
2. **Financial Exploitation:**With access to your Apple iPhone 7, thieves may attempt to get stored payment methods.
3. **Identity Theft:** Stolen iPhones often contain a wealth of sensitive information. This makes them a prime target for identity theft.
4. **Compromised Social Media Accounts:** Using social media applications on your smartphone carries potential risks in the event of device loss. Unauthorized access to your social media accounts can lead to many problems, including misinformation and compromised privacy.
